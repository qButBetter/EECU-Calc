What do I need to know?

The 50/30/20 Budget

The 50/30/20 budget is a rule-of-thumb way to tell right off the bat if your spending may need to be improved. It's okay to have a slight deviation of this rule, just have a way to determine if your spending is helpful for your lifestyle.

Key Finance Terms

It's always good to know what you're doing in all situations. Here's a couple terms that you may need to know for this calculator. “Gross” income is what your salary is before taxes. “Net” income is what your salary is after taxes. Lastly, “difference” means the amount of money you have after all expenses are taken out.
